Landlord Rules

  • Respect all clan members. Toxicity will not be tolerated.
  • No stealing from clan bases or teammates under any circumstances.
  • Be active. If you’re offline for more than 5 days without notice, you may be kicked.
  • Communicate in voice chat during raids and PvP.
  • Follow leadership calls during raids, even if you disagree.
  • Share loot fairly — don’t hoard high-tier gear without reason.
  • No cheating, scripting, or using exploits. Instant ban and report to server admin.
  • Always replace what you take from base loot rooms when possible (farm, craft, donate).
  • Defend the base when under raid — drop what you’re doing and help out.
  • Have fun and represent the clan with pride!
